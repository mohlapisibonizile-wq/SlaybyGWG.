Our Business Model

Royal Roots Hair operates as a beauty and hair business that generates revenue through the sale of high-quality hair products and professional hair services. Our goal is to provide affordable luxury while maintaining excellent customer satisfaction.

Revenue Streams

  • Sale of wigs and hair bundles
  • Hair installation services
  • Styling and maintenance services

Target Market

Our target market includes young women, students, and working professionals in Maseru who are interested in fashionable and high-quality hair products and services.

Value Proposition

We offer premium quality hair at affordable prices, combined with professional services that enhance beauty and confidence. Our products are stylish, durable, and suitable for different occasions.

Sales Channels

  • In-store services and walk-in clients
  • Social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp)
  • Online promotions and bookings

Customer Relationships

We build strong relationships with our customers by providing friendly service, personalized styling advice, and consistent quality. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.

Future Growth

Royal Roots Hair aims to expand by introducing more hair product varieties, offering home services, and growing our online presence to reach more customers across Lesotho.
